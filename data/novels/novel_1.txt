John Mercer was born in London in a poor neighborhood. His childhood was marked by financial hardship, 
and his family often struggled to afford basic necessities.

Despite these difficulties, John was determined to improve his life. He studied hard in school and 
earned a scholarship to attend medical college. During his studies, he showed exceptional dedication 
and empathy toward patients.

After graduating, John became a licensed doctor and began working at a public hospital. His early 
experiences with poverty influenced his decision to serve underprivileged communities. Throughout 
the novel, his background is repeatedly cited as the motivation behind his career choices.
